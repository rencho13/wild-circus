About Us

Created in Marseille in 1932 by the brothers Marius and César Ollivier, specialists in equestrian art, the wild circus throngs American soil for the first time in 1975 for a series of performances. Very quickly, the back and forth between the two countries multiply before a final installation in the United States in 1984 under the leadership of Panisse Ollivier, the son of Marius.

In the spring of 2018, like many show companies, the wild circus faces difficulties and must stop its tour. Passionate, a man of challenges, Gilbert Edelstein can not bring himself to the end of this circus jewel, which he has helped to develop and support for more than 30 years. Surrounded by his team, he decides that Pinder will be present in Paris as every year. He entrusted the operational management to his right arm Claude Vonner and the artistic direction to Judicaël Vattier, a young artist and director already recognized. Together, they make the bet to propose a completely new show that gives pride of place to the new generation of French artists, all in the respect of the traditional circus, true DNA of Wild Circus.
